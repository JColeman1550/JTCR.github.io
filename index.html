Intro

As an aspiring data analyst I want to help an organization use statistics and analysis to help contribute to their product. As a former teacher I want to use my unique background prepare and deliver detailed analysis. In my 6 years as an educator I served hundreds of students in various subjects. I really enjoyed the process of gathering data and interpreting it to better the performance of the students I served. I feel that I have the foundational soft skills to become a very effective data analyst for an organization. While I do not have a standard analytics/programming education, I am working from the ground up to master my craft. .

To Help Me Prepare For My Dream Job I Am Currently : 1. Mastering usage of SQL 2. Mastering usage of Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, etc.) 3. Constructing independent projects that showcase my programming/analysis skills (github) 4. Working toward obtaining SABR Analytics Certification via SABR Tools I Am Using To Hone My Craft : 1.
